— My recommendation

Option 3 gets Padel built now — with nothing upfront and no permanent change to your monthly.

Whichever path you pick, the build is identical: one dedicated team, Padel live in around four weeks, the full £6,000 covered. The only real difference is how the cost sits in your cash flow.

If you'd rather settle it once and forget it, Pay in Full (1) is the cleanest. If you'd like the spend to track visible progress, the Milestone Split (2) releases it in three stages against delivery. For most situations, though, Rolling it into the Retainer (3) is the easiest yes: nothing to find today, the build starts immediately, and the cost folds into three months of a slightly higher retainer before dropping straight back to £3,500. You get the product now and feel the cost gently.

Staying on the retainer (4) is on the table for transparency — no new spend at all. But that team was scoped for marketing and updates, not a ground-up build, so pushing the full build through it drags delivery to ~12 weeks and slows your day-to-day content and marketing while it runs. It's the cheapest line on paper and the slowest to a live product by a wide margin — every week Padel isn't live is a week it isn't earning.
