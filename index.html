My recommendation

Option 03 gets all three sports built — without an £18k hit and without straining your team.

If pure speed is the deciding factor, the Full Sprint (01) is the cleanest answer: pay once, full team in parallel, all three live in eight weeks.

For most situations, though, Scaling the Retainer (03) is the smarter balance. You spread the entire build across four months with no lump sum, you get a dedicated team built for the job, and your existing update and marketing work never gets stretched thin. Adding the optional PLUS keeps a dev on hand for changes as they surface, so the product keeps improving mid-build. Retainer Absorb (02) is on the table for transparency — but that team was scoped for updates and optimisations, not heavy builds, so pushing a full three-sport build through it drags delivery out to six months. It's the slowest route to market by a wide margin.
