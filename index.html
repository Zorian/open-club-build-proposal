— My recommendation

Option 3 builds all three sports — without an £18k hit, and without straining your team.

If pure speed is the deciding factor, the Full Sprint (1) is the cleanest answer: pay once, full team in parallel, all three live in eight weeks.

For most situations, though, Scaling the Retainer (3) is the smarter balance. You spread the entire build across four months with no lump sum, you get a dedicated team built for the job, and your existing update and marketing work never gets stretched thin. The optional PLUS keeps a dev on hand for changes as they surface, so the product keeps improving mid-build. Retainer Absorb (2) is on the table for transparency — but that team was scoped for updates, not heavy builds, so pushing a full build through it drags delivery to six months. It's the slowest route to market by a wide margin.
